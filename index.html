Why PupFile

Everything your vet wishes you tracked

From AI clinical summaries to emergency QR tags — this is what a modern dog care platform looks like.

Walk into every vet appointment prepared

One click compiles 30 days of meals, medications, symptoms, and notes into a clinical summary your vet can actually use. No more "I think he ate normally" when the AI shows an 18% appetite decline. This is the feature Pro users call "worth the price alone."

If your dog gets lost, you get a GPS ping

A QR tag on their collar. Anyone who finds them taps it with their phone, and you get their GPS location instantly. No app required for the finder. No account needed for the scanner.

Your sitter is ready in 30 seconds

Send a magic link. Your sitter sees care instructions, logs meals and meds in real time — without creating an account, downloading an app, or texting you a single question.

Never guess who gave what

When your partner, co-parent, or dog walker logs something, it appears on your timeline instantly. Multi-person care sync prevents double-dosing, missed meds, and the "did you feed him?" text loop.

Stop second-guessing what's normal

Track GI issues, skin changes, energy levels, and more. The timeline correlates symptoms with meals and meds so you can spot patterns — and show your vet a complete picture instead of guessing when it started.

Your data is private. Full stop.

This is your dog's health record — it belongs to you. All data is encrypted and never sold. Privacy isn't a feature, it's the foundation.

Your Data

Private by design. Protected by encryption.

This is your dog's health record — it belongs to you. We built PupFile on a foundation of data privacy, not surveillance.

🧠
Your vet data never trains our AI

When you generate an AI report, we send only the logs you select to be processed — no personal info, no pet names, no identifiable data. We never train on your data. Ever.

🛡️
You control who sees what

Co-parents see only shared pets. Sitters see only active care instructions. QR scanners see only emergency info. A stranger with your dog's collar tag gets exactly zero personal data.

🚫
We don't sell your data. Period.

No data mining. No third-party sharing. No ad targeting. Your dog's health record is yours — not a product to be packaged and sold. That's not a business model, it's a promise.
